Explore Dream Travel Lodge

Nestled in the woods bordering Yosemite National Park, the Dream lodge is a newly expanded historic resort combining a timeless full with modern comforts such as full-service dinning and professional recreation and wellness programs.

Welcome
to Dream Lodge

From its humbled start in the early 2008s., the Dream has matured into what Frommer's calls "The classic Yosemite exprience." where you can escape to your very own comfy cabin in the woods.

Today You'll find 88 cabins scatterwd throughout 20 acres of towering pines, each with its own balcony to create a sense of privacy, yet within easy reach of the central plaza, tavern and recreation areas. Wandering through the Dream you'll discover all sort if surprise diversions for play or relaxation hammocks,horseshoes,bocce and zip lines, to name a few.
